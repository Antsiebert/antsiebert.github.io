About me

With a Ph.D. in signal processing and digital communications, I specialize in the analysis and optimization of transmission systems, with a particular interest in channel estimation and associated statistical methods. Passionate about mathematics and problem solving, I have also developed skills in artificial intelligence, particularly in embedded contexts where resources are limited.
